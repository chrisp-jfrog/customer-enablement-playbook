  <h2>How to Use These Resources</h2>

  <p>
    Every playbook in this repository is designed to be used directly — copy a checklist into your CRM, paste an email template into your mail client, or assign a runbook step as a task. No setup required.
  </p>

  <p>
    All resources cross-reference each other. The onboarding checklist links to specific email templates at each stage. The escalation runbook points to the relevant email template for each severity level. You should never have to hunt for the next thing to do.
  </p>
