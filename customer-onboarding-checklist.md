# Customer Onboarding Checklist

> **Purpose:** Ensure every new customer reaches their first value milestone consistently and on time.
> **Owner:** Customer Success Manager
> **Timeline:** Days 1–30

---

## How to Use This Checklist

Work through each phase in order. Mark items complete as you go. Flag blockers in your CRM and loop in the relevant team using the [Escalation Runbook](../guides/escalation-runbook.md) if needed. Use the [Email Templates](../guides/email-templates.md) for all outbound customer communication.

---

## Phase 1 — Pre-Kickoff (Before Day 1)

**Internal preparation — complete before the customer's kickoff call.**

- [ ] CRM record created and account fields populated (tier, ARR, use case, key contacts)
- [ ] Kickoff call scheduled within 5 business days of contract signature
- [ ] Internal kickoff completed with AE, SE, and Support to transfer context
- [ ] Success plan draft created with initial goals and 30/60/90 milestones
- [ ] Customer Slack channel or shared workspace created (if applicable)
- [ ] Welcome email sent *(use [Welcome Email template](../guides/email-templates.md#welcome-email))*

---

## Phase 2 — Kickoff (Day 1–3)

**Goal: Align on expectations, relationships, and a clear first milestone.**

- [ ] Kickoff call held — agenda shared 24 hours in advance
- [ ] Customer stakeholders mapped: Executive Sponsor, Day-to-Day Champion, Technical Lead
- [ ] Primary use case and success criteria confirmed and documented
- [ ] Technical requirements reviewed with SE (integrations, SSO, data, permissions)
- [ ] Onboarding timeline agreed on and shared with customer
- [ ] Next steps documented and sent within 24 hours of kickoff *(use [Kickoff Follow-Up template](../guides/email-templates.md#kickoff-follow-up))*

---

## Phase 3 — Setup & Configuration (Day 3–14)

**Goal: Get the product live in the customer's environment.**

- [ ] Admin access provisioned for customer's technical lead
- [ ] Users invited and roles assigned
- [ ] SSO / SAML configured (if applicable)
- [ ] Integrations connected and tested (CRM, ticketing, data sources)
- [ ] Initial data or content migrated (if applicable)
- [ ] Core workflows configured to match customer's stated use case
- [ ] Configuration review call held — SE confirmed sign-off
- [ ] Setup complete confirmation sent *(use [Setup Complete template](../guides/email-templates.md#setup-complete))*

---

## Phase 4 — Enablement & Adoption (Day 14–30)

**Goal: Drive user adoption and confirm value realization.**

- [ ] Admin training session completed
- [ ] End-user training session completed (or self-serve learning path assigned)
- [ ] Knowledge base / documentation links shared with customer
- [ ] First 10 active users confirmed in product analytics
- [ ] Customer's first key workflow completed (as defined in success plan)
- [ ] Check-in call held at Day 14 to address friction or blockers
- [ ] Day 30 business review scheduled

---

## Phase 5 — Day 30 Review

**Goal: Confirm first value, review health, and set 60/90-day goals.**

- [ ] Usage and adoption metrics pulled from analytics dashboard
- [ ] Success criteria reviewed against Day 1 targets
- [ ] Open issues and support tickets reviewed and resolved or tracked
- [ ] Customer sentiment confirmed (NPS or qualitative check-in)
- [ ] 60/90-day goals documented in success plan
- [ ] Day 30 summary sent to customer and exec sponsor *(use [30-Day Review template](../guides/email-templates.md#30-day-review))*

---

## Escalation Triggers

Immediately open an escalation if any of the following occur:

| Signal | Action |
|---|---|
| Kickoff not scheduled within 5 business days | Escalate to CSM Manager |
| Technical blocker unresolved after 48 hours | Escalate to SE Lead + Support |
| No admin login activity by Day 7 | Escalate to CSM Manager; send re-engagement email |
| Customer expresses dissatisfaction at any stage | Escalate to CSM Manager + VP CS within 24 hours |
| Integration or SSO failure by Day 14 | Escalate to Engineering via Support |

See the full [Escalation Runbook](../guides/escalation-runbook.md) for procedures.
