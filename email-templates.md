## Welcome Email

**When to send:** Within 24 hours of contract signature, before kickoff.
**Sender:** Assigned CSM

---

**Subject:** Welcome to [Product] — your next steps

Hi [First Name],

Welcome aboard! I'm [CSM Name], and I'll be your Customer Success Manager as you get started with [Product].

My goal is to make sure you hit the ground running and get real value quickly. Here's what to expect over the next few weeks:

- **This week:** We'll schedule your kickoff call to align on goals and walk through the setup process.
- **Weeks 2–3:** Your team gets the product configured and integrated.
- **Week 4:** We'll check in to confirm everything is working and you're seeing the results you signed up for.

To get us started, could you reply with a few times that work for a 45-minute kickoff call? Alternatively, grab a time directly on my calendar: [Calendar Link].

Looking forward to working with you.

[CSM Name]
[Title] | [Company]
[Email] | [Phone]

## SEV-1 Incident Acknowledgment

**When to send:** Within 30 minutes of a SEV-1 issue being identified.
**Sender:** Assigned CSM
**CC:** CSM Manager

---

**Subject:** [URGENT] We're aware of an issue affecting your account — [Date]

Hi [First Name],

I'm reaching out because we've identified an issue affecting [describe impact — e.g., "your integration between [Product] and [System]"]. I want you to hear from me directly before this impacts your team further.

**What's happening:** [Brief, plain-language description of the issue.]

**Current status:** Our engineering team is actively investigating. We have a dedicated team working on this right now.

**Immediate impact:** [Describe what is and isn't working.]

**What you should do in the meantime:** [Workaround, if any, or "No action needed on your end."]

I will send you an update by [Time — per SLA, every 2 hours]. If you need to reach me immediately, call or text me at [Phone Number].

I'm sorry for the disruption and appreciate your patience.

[CSM Name]
[Direct Phone]

## Template Usage Notes

- Always personalize the opening sentence — never send a template cold.
- Replace all `[bracketed fields]` before sending. A missed bracket is unprofessional.
- For urgent/escalation emails, always CC your manager.
- Log every customer email sent in the CRM under the account's activity timeline.
- If a response is needed, set a follow-up reminder in your CRM for 1 business day.
